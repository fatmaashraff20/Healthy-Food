Safty Food

A healthy diet is essential for good health and nutrition. It protects you against many chronic noncommunicable diseases, such as heart disease, diabetes and cancer. Eating a variety of foods and consuming less salt, sugars and saturated and industrially-produced trans-fats, are essential for healthy diet.

Healthy Food

Healthy food refers to food choices that provide essential nutrients, vitamins, and minerals while supporting overall well-being and maintaining a balanced diet.

Lean Proteins

Opt for lean protein sources such as poultry, fish, beans, legumes, tofu, and low-fat dairy products. These provide essential amino acids for growth, repair, and maintenance of body tissues.

Fruits and Vegetables

Include a wide range of fruits and vegetables in your diet as they are rich in vitamins, minerals, and fiber. Aim for at least five servings of fruits and vegetables per day.

Limit Added Sugars

Minimize the consumption of foods and drinks high in added sugars, such as sugary beverages, desserts, and processed snacks. Instead, satisfy your sweet tooth with whole fruits.

3 Step For Healthy Eating

01

Read food labels When buying packaged foods, read the labels carefully. Pay attention to the ingredients list and nutritional information, especially regarding added sugars, unhealthy fats, and sodium content.

02

Cooking your own meals gives you control over the ingredients and portion sizes. It allows you to use healthier cooking methods, such as grilling and reduces reliance on processed foods or take-out meals.

03

Stay hydrated Drink an adequate amount of water throughout the day. Water helps maintain proper bodily functions, aids digestion, and keeps you feeling refreshed. Limit sugary beverages and opt for water as your primary drink.
